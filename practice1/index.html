My collection of Soccer souvenirs

Why this collection means so much to me

Over the years, I have built a collection of soccer souvenirs that shows my passion for soccer. From autographed jerseys to limited-edition soccer balls, every item in this collection holds a special place in my heart.

Making a tough choice

I have to sell some of those things I love to pay my tuition bill. I had to sell some of my limited-edition jerseys, but I was lucky and got to keep my soccer ball used in a match back in my home country Syria.
