History of Real Madrid

Real Madrid's history began in 1902, when people who loved football and students from the Institución Libre de Enseñanza founded the club.

The club's name "Real" (Spanish for Royal) was given by King Alfonso XIII in 1920, adding the crown to its logo.

Historical Achievements

First La Liga Title: 1932

First European Cup: 1956

FIFA Club of the 20th Century: Winner
