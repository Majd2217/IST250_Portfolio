Welcome to Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. The club is one of the most successful and popular football teams in the world.

Founded in 1902, Real Madrid has become one of the biggest if not the biggest football teams, winning a lot of domestic and international titles.
