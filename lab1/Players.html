Legendary Players

Throughout its history, Real Madrid has been home to some of football's greatest players.

Notable Legends

Alfredo Di Stéfano - The Complete Footballer, started Real Madrid's dominance in the Eurpean Cup

Cristiano Ronaldo - All-time Top Scorer, Saved the club after a 12 year drought with no Champions League trophy

Raúl González - Club Icon, One of the best to play for Madrid and came from their academy La Fàbrica

Iker Casillas - Legendary Goalkeeper, one of the best goalkeeprs to play football ever.
